How to make Chicken Wings

Here is a brief overview of what you can expect when you bake chicken wings at home:

Ingredients

  • Oil: Olive oil locks in the moisture and gives the seasonings something to stick to.
  • Seasonings: The baked wings are flavored with a blend of fresh garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  • Wings: Youâ€™ll need 10 chicken wings for this recipe, which makes about two servings.

Steps

  1. Combine the oil and seasonings in a bag and shake to blend.
  2. Add the chicken wings to the bag, seal and shake to coat.
  3. Arrange the wings on a baking sheet and bake until crisp and cooked through.
